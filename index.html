FAQ

Who can participate in FUTUREHACK 2018

Non-experienced student coders and hackathon starters only because it is a starter’s hackathon. As long as you are passionate in coding and have creative ideas, you are welcome to join!

What are the rules for forming a team?

A team can consist of any student from any university in Malaysia. The minimum number of teammates is 3, and each team must not exceed 5 members. We recommend a well-balanced team should have a designer, passionate soon-to-be coder, marketer.

What will we be building in FutureHack 2018?

Participants are expected to build websites and web apps that is able to solve problem expressed by the theme.



What is our theme “Hacking University Life” about?

We hope to let participants create products that will simplify or enrich a student’s daily life.

What are the things do I need to prepare for the hackathon?
  • Bring your own laptop installed with necessary software development tools.
  • Please bring along your jacket as it could be cold while you hack throughout the night.
  • (Optional) Bring sleeping bags for a late night’s rest
How long is the event?

It will be a 24-hour competitive hackathon. Registration starts at 9.00 a.m. (Saturday 14th April) and the real hacking session will start at 11.00 a.m. The hacking ends at 11.00 a.m. (Sunday 15th April).

Do I need to pay any fees to join FutureHack 2018?

The answer is… NO. We want to make it free for students from all walks of life to join us and learn without barriers. However, a deposit of RMXX is required. Deposit will be refunded during event registration process.
